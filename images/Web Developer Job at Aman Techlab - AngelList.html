Aman Tech Lab is a captive consultancy and IT services startup at the seed stage that caters to all I.T. related requirements of a set of in-house businesses/companies. Foundation of the firm is still being laid and team members are being aggressively headhunted, recruited and inducted. The in-house businesses that it caters to are as diverse as ecommerce to production and online sales of high fashion women’s wear. Each of the businesses offers a proposition that is unique and is centred on key innovations. Aman Tech Lab assists them by deploying software solutions or developing solutions for them from scratch.

By the end of 2018, we expect Aman Techlab to grow to 100+ IT developers, engineers, designers, architects and analysts. Several innovations are expected to emerge out of the laboratory that we are committed to turning into a hotbed of unleashed creativity over a period of time. In future, we may explore and consider offering our services in the open market.

Job Description

Aman Techlab is building a top notch team of technology enthusiasts who are deeply passionate about creating immense value and delivering superlative performance. You will take ownership of what you build and will be an integral part of the value chain comprising of design, development and digital marketing.

• Minimum 2 years of experience in developing web applications and ecommerce portals

• Excellent knowledge of PHP, MySQL, AJAX, jQuery, JavaScript, HTML/HTML5 & CSS

• Excellent DB design skills

• Excellent development skills in Wordpress CMS

• Understanding of cross browsers & mobile compatibility

• Experience with AWS (Amazon Web Services) related services preferred

• API consumption and generation for mobile app (XML, JSON, RPC, SOAP, Rest)

• Experience with version control systems (Git, SVN)

• Must have a thorough understanding of MVC

• Sound Knowledge of Payment Gateway

• Knowledge about the server administration
